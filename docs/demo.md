# Demo Flow (15 minutes)

The goal is one moment: they see the OS produce a real, sourced number from their own context. Show, do not tell.

## 0 to 2 min · The problem
One line. "Every tool you have forgets your business by tomorrow." Show a blank folder. "This is the whole product. A folder."

## 2 to 6 min · Setup
Run `/setup` live. Paste their website. Drop one doc. Watch it build the vault, seed the ledger, and write a first deliverable. Call out the report card: solid, thin, missing.

## 6 to 9 min · A real workflow
Run one delivery skill against their context. `/seo-audit` on their site, or `/lead-research` on a target account. Show the draft and the ledger row it wrote, with a source.

## 9 to 12 min · The compounding
Open `Memory/kpi-ledger.md`. Show the rows. Run `/business-review`. Show the scorecard and the trend. "This is the part no other tool has. The history is yours, and it proves the work."

## 12 to 14 min · The firewall (agency demo only)
Open two client folders. Try to reference one from the other. Run `scripts/check-firewall.sh`. Show it fail, then pass. "Your clients are sealed off from each other, and we can prove it."

## 14 to 15 min · The ask
"You keep every file. Fixed price, refundable if the first deliverable is not useful." Point to `docs/pricing.md`. One next step: start the Self-Serve, or book the Done-With-You week.
