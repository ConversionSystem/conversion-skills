# Quickstart

A populated, governed business vault with a first deliverable in 10 minutes. Zero infra.

## What you need
- Claude (Claude Code or Cowork).
- An empty folder you control, ideally inside a synced drive so it is backed up and shareable.
- Git. Optional, recommended. It is your history and your safety net.

## Run it
1. Point Claude at the empty folder.
2. Run `/setup`. Pick a profile: Solo, Team, or Agency.
3. Paste your site, drop a few docs (deck, pricing, about). Setup reads them first, so it confirms what it can infer instead of asking.
4. Answer a few skippable questions. One-liner, offers, ICP, voice, top goal.
5. Setup scaffolds the vault, seeds your context and your first KPI baselines, and hands you a first deliverable and a report card (solid, thin, missing).

## The daily loop
- Morning: `/daily`. Today's brief and Top 3.
- As things happen: `/meeting-capture`, `/pipeline-update`, or drop anything into `Inbox/`.
- Friday: `/weekly-review`. Monthly: `/business-review`, which proves the trend.
- Now and then: `/optimizer` to keep the vault clean.

## Where things live
Open `CLAUDE.md` at the vault root. It is the map. Every kind of information has one home, listed in its routing table.
